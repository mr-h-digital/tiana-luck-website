Before Dawn

Supernatural Fiction Mystery Horror

The fog started just after midnight.

When Yuki's car dies on a mountain road and her phone goes dark, she finds herself in a town with no signal, no mirrors, and one rule she'll struggle to follow: never leave your house after dawn.

As strange shadows press against her windows and reflections show faces that aren't hers, Yuki realises the rules weren't put in place to keep her inside — they were put there to keep something out.
